Our Services

Summary of our services:

We offer professional wholesale and retail pre-order services for those looking to import quality but affordable goods and products from China & Turkey to Ghana.
With three (3) years of experience, we also offer training sessions for up and coming individuals.

Find out how much more beautiful life can be by browsing through our services.


Sourcing of Products

VinCee imports takes care of all the logistics, including sourcing the products, handling international shipping, and managing customs clearance.
By leveraging our network of global suppliers, we ensure that clients get their desired products in a timely and efficient manner.
By handling these processes on behalf of our clients, we aim to alleviate the stress and complexities associated with cross-border transactions.

Wholesale Pre-Order

We assist individuals and businesses in bringing goods and products from China & Turkey into Ghana.
You can order in large quantities and resell them. Wholesale prices are relatively lower than retail prices.

Retail Pre-Order

With deep a understanding of import laws and regulations, we ensure a smooth and hassle-free process for our clients.
You can pre-order products in small quantities for personal use. Product prices are relatively cheap as compared to local market prices even after adding shipping fees.

Training Services

We train young and ambitious individuals to source, purchase and ship goods from China & Turkey to Ghana. We have bonafide suppliers that we can connect you for all your importation needs.
